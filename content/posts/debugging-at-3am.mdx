# The 3AM Debugging Loop

We've all been there. It's late, the bug is elusive, and you've tried everything.

## Step 1: Walk Away
Sometimes the best debugging tool is a good night's sleep or a short walk.

## Step 2: Rubber Ducking
Explain your code to a rubber duck (or a colleague).

## Step 3: Check the Basics
Is it a typo? A missing environment variable?

Stay persistent!
